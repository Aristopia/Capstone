Step 1: Restart Computer


click F2 on startup to get to BIOS


Under Secure Boot > Secure Boot Enable, uncheck the 'Secure Boot Enable' checkbox






click 'yes' on the pop-up






Under General > Advanced Boot Options, check the box 'Enable Legacy Option ROMs'






click 'yes' on the pop-up






Under Genereal > Boot Sequence, check the 'Legacy External Devices' button











click the 'Apply' Button in the bottom right






click 'OK'






Next, insert your usb with the flashed Kali Linux OS, and click 'Exit' on the bottom right to boot into Kali Linux

When finished with Kali, restart computer and go back into BIOS

Follow the same steps, but in reverse order, then click 'Apply' and 'Exit' to return to your original OS
